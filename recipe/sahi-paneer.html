Sahi Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 225-250 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • ½ cup curd (plain yogurt)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • ⅛ teaspoon turmeric (optional)
  • ¾ to 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 pinch saffron strands (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • ¾ cup water
  • 3 tablespoons cream (optional)
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon ghee in a pan and fry cashews, green cardamom, and almonds until light golden. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add 1 onion, green chili, ginger, and coriander leaves. Fry until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the curd and whisk well. Cook until the mixture thickens, then add 1 cup water and cook until the gravy is smooth.
  4. Add the fried cashews, almonds, and spices (red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt). Mix well.
  5. Add the paneer cubes and cook until they are coated with the gravy.
  6. If desired, add 3 tablespoons cream to enhance the richness.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or roti.
