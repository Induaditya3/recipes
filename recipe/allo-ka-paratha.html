Allo Ka Paratha

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups whole wheat flour (can sub half with all-purpose flour for a crisper paratha)
  • ½ tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Water for kneading
  • 2 pounds potatoes
  • ½ tsp cayenne
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garlic (crushed or ½ tsp garlic powder)
  • 1 tsp ginger (crushed or ½ tsp ginger powder)
  • 1 tsp chaat masala (or amchur)
  • ½ tsp garam masala (optional)
  • 2 tbsp cilantro (finely chopped)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Make the dough: Place all ingredients except water in a bowl or stand mixer. Trickle in water slowly, kneading as you go, until you have a stiff but pliable dough. Set aside for at least 30 minutes to relax
  2. Prepare the potato filling: Boil potatoes until tender, then mash and mix with cayenne, turmeric, garlic, ginger, chaat masala, and garam masala (if using). Season with salt to taste.
  3. Assemble and cook the parathas: Divide the dough into 4-6 equal parts. Roll out each part into a thin circle. Place a tablespoon or two of the potato filling in the center. Fold the dough over the filling to form a triangle or square shape, and press the edges together to seal. Cook the parathas in a non-stick skillet or tava over medium heat for 1-2 minutes on each side, until golden brown and crispy.
